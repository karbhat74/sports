About Us

Welcome to the Department of Physical Education at JSS Academy of Technical Education, Bengaluru. Our mission is to nurture the holistic development of students through a robust sports and physical education program.

Our Vision

To attain the highest position in the sports arena at the state level through consistent and sustainable development programs.

Our Mission

  • Providing opportunities to participate in recreational and competitive sporting activities.
  • Creating state-of-the-art infrastructure for diverse sports and games.
  • Fostering better human relations through sports and games.
  • Encouraging discipline, leadership qualities, and teamwork.

Key Achievements

We take pride in hosting and participating in numerous inter-collegiate, zonal, and state-level events, with our teams consistently excelling across various sports.
